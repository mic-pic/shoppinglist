Recipe of the month

Lasagna with Pesto, Ricotta, and Cherry Tomatoes

70 minutes

Easy

  • Pan
  • Oven
  • Tall container
  • Baking dish
  • Stove
  • Knife
  • Cutting board
  • Immersion blender

Ingredients for 2 persons

For the pesto

  • 1.5 bunches of basil
  • 1 bunch of arugula
  • 50 g pine nuts
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 25 g grated parmesan
  • 75 ml olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Further

  • 400 g cherry tomatoes
  • 400 g ricotta
  • 5 lasagna sheets
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Preparation

1.

For the pesto, wash the basil and arugula and shake dry. Pluck the basil leaves. Toast the pine nuts in a pan without oil and then let them cool. Peel the garlic and finely blend it together with the remaining ingredients in a tall container using an immersion blender.

2.

Preheat the oven (top/bottom heat: 200°C/fan: 175°C). Wash the tomatoes, pat dry, and quarter them. Season the ricotta with salt and pepper. In a baking dish, layer the lasagna alternately with ricotta, pesto, tomatoes, and lasagna sheets. Bake on the middle rack for about 45 minutes.
